Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my Zylobin app password?

To reset your password, open the Zylobin app and tap on "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your registered email address and follow the instructions sent to your email to create a new password.

How can I cancel a scheduled pickup?

You can cancel a scheduled pickup through the app up to 4 hours before the scheduled time. Go to "My Pickups" in the app, select the pickup you want to cancel, and tap on "Cancel Pickup". Follow the prompts to confirm the cancellation.

What should I do if the collector didn't pick up my waste?

If your scheduled pickup was missed, please report it through the app by going to "My Pickups" and selecting "Report Issue" for the specific pickup. Our team will investigate and follow up with you within 24 hours.

How do I change my collection address?

To change your collection address, go to your account settings by tapping on your profile icon. Select "Addresses" and then "Edit" next to the address you want to change, or "Add New Address" to add a new location. Follow the prompts to save your updated address information.

How do I sort my waste properly?

Proper waste sorting is essential for effective recycling. Check the "Waste Guide" section in the app for detailed instructions on how to sort different types of waste. You can also scan item barcodes using the app's camera feature to get specific disposal instructions.
