Frequently Asked Questions About Waste Collection

What types of waste can I collect with Zylobin?

Zylobin supports collection and tracking of most common materials including plastic, paper, cardboard, glass, metal, e-waste, textiles, and in some locations, organic waste. The app will show which materials are accepted at nearby collection points.

How much does the Zylobin collector app cost?

The Zylobin collector app is completely free to download and use for all waste collectors. There are no fees for logging your collections or finding drop-off points. Only registered collection centers require subscription plans.

How do I find collection points near me?

The Zylobin app includes an interactive map showing all registered collection points in your area. You can filter by distance, accepted materials, and operating hours to find the most convenient location to drop off your collected waste.

How do I register my business as a collection point?

Recycling businesses can register through our Collection Point Portal. After verification of your business credentials and location details, you'll gain access to our platform and can choose from our subscription plans to manage your collection point(s).
