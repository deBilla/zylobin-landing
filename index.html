Frequently Asked Questions About Recycling

What types of waste can I recycle with Zylobin?

Zylobin supports recycling of most common materials including plastic, paper, cardboard, glass, metal, e-waste, textiles, and in some locations, organic waste. The app will show which materials are accepted in your area.

How much does the Zylobin consumer app cost?

The Zylobin consumer app is completely free to download and use for all basic recycling management features. Premium features may be available through in-app subscription options.

How quickly can I get a waste pickup scheduled?

Pickup availability varies by location and collector partner schedules. In most urban areas, you can schedule a pickup within 24-48 hours, while same-day pickups may be available in some regions.

How do I become a waste collector partner?

Waste collection businesses can register through our Collector Portal. After verification of your business credentials and service areas, you'll gain access to our platform and can choose from our subscription plans.
