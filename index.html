Our Solutions

Zylobin Consumer App

Our intuitive mobile application helps you:

  • Create and manage digital waste bins by category
  • Record recyclable items with a simple scan
  • Find nearby collection centers
  • Schedule convenient pickup times
  • Track your recycling history and environmental impact
  • Earn rewards for consistent recycling habits

Zylobin Collector Platform

Our subscription-based B2B platform empowers waste collectors with:

  • Real-time waste item tracking and analytics
  • Smart route optimization for efficient collections
  • Dashboard for monitoring user activity and waste accumulation
  • Integration with your existing collection operations
